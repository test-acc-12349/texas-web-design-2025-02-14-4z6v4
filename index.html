About Texas Web Design

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate Texas tech entrepreneurs, Texas Web Design began with a simple mission: to provide local businesses with world-class web solutions at affordable prices. What started as a small studio in Austin has grown into the state's premier web design agency, serving hundreds of satisfied clients across Texas.

Our core values of innovation, integrity, and client success drive everything we do. We believe in delivering more than just websites - we create digital experiences that transform businesses and drive real results. Our team combines cutting-edge technology with traditional Texas values of hard work and exceptional service.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your hosting package?

Our enterprise hosting includes SSL certificates, daily backups, 24/7 monitoring, DDoS protection, and global CDN distribution. We guarantee 99.9% uptime and provide round-the-clock support.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple websites can be done in as little as 1 week, while more complex e-commerce solutions may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including security updates, content updates, performance optimization, and technical support. Our team is available via phone, email, and chat during business hours.

Do you provide SEO services?

Yes, all our websites come with basic SEO optimization. We also offer advanced SEO packages including keyword research, content optimization, link building, and monthly performance reporting.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans with a 50% deposit to start and the remainder due upon completion. Monthly payment plans are available for larger projects. We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers.
